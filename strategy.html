⑦ Data & method
Corpus. 286 Westin/Whiteland posts (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn) + 135 third-party items (Reddit, news) — every one sentiment-scored and theme-tagged with Claude judgment, deduplicated by URL. Rival side: 697 active ads across 18 rivals from the Meta Ad Library, plus 20 real organic rival posts (Signature Global, Oberoi, DLF) that got past the login walls.

What sentiment means per source. Westin's sentiment is true audience tone — it includes third-party Reddit/news chatter (which is where the only real negatives live: possession −0.60, price −0.47). Rival organic sentiment (Signature/Oberoi) is also audience-weighted. Rival ad cells (faded, ad) are self-authored messaging tone, not audience — shown for completeness only.

Honest gap. Rival organic sentiment at scale is not reliably scrapeable — Instagram/Facebook anti-bot + login walls block the standard actors (only ~20 rival posts leaked across 6 page-IDs). A full rival-sentiment feed needs premium residential-proxy actors, or pivot to third-party chatter (mentions, reviews, forums) which IS scrapeable. CPC / bid / budget is never obtainable from any tool — it is private auction data.

Themes. The 8 priority real-estate themes from the strategy doc + a 9th "luxury/branded" category axis (both sides have heavy data on it; excluding it would distort).
